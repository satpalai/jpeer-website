Our carers in Dandenong speak your language

Dandenong is one of Australia's most culturally diverse communities. Our care team reflects that diversity. We provide care in Hindi, Urdu, Tamil, Malay, and English — so your loved one can express their needs, share their feelings, and feel truly at ease with the person supporting them each day.

For many older residents, English is a second or third language. As people age and experience cognitive changes, their first language becomes even more important. A carer who can hold a conversation in Hindi or Tamil isn't just a convenience — it's a form of respect.

Why Dandenong families choose J.PEER Health

We understand this community deeply. Dandenong's Indian, Sri Lankan, and Cambodian communities have distinct care cultures — extended family involvement, specific dietary practices, and deeply held values around dignity and privacy. Our care plans reflect all of this.

We're close to Dandenong Hospital. When a loved one is discharged from hospital and needs post-hospital care, we can often begin support within 24 to 48 hours — reducing the risk of re-admission and giving the whole family peace of mind.

We come to the Dandenong Market and beyond. Whether it's accompanying your loved one to the market on a Saturday morning or helping them stay connected to the community activities they've always loved near Dandenong Creek Parklands — we build care around their life, not the other way around.

Common questions about home care in Dandenong

How do I get home care in Dandenong?

Getting started is simple. Call J.PEER Health on 0469 371 121 or fill in our online enquiry form. We'll have a friendly conversation about your needs, explain your funding options — including the Support at Home program — and arrange a care assessment at a time that suits you. Care can often begin within days.

What areas near Dandenong does J.PEER Health serve?

We provide home care across Greater Dandenong and surrounding suburbs including Noble Park, Springvale, Hallam, Endeavour Hills, Narre Warren, and Berwick. Call us to confirm coverage of your specific street.

Can J.PEER Health provide care in languages other than English in Dandenong?

Yes. Our Dandenong care team speaks Hindi, Urdu, Tamil, Malay, and English. For older community members who are most comfortable in their first language, language-matched care isn't a luxury — it's essential for dignity and genuine connection. Call us and we'll match you with a carer who speaks your language.
