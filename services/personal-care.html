What We Provide

Everything covered in personal care

From the first wash in the morning to the last routine at night, our carers are trained to handle every aspect of daily personal care — thoughtfully, unhurriedly, and without fuss.

Bathing & showering

Full shower or bed bath assistance, including hair washing, with full attention to safety and personal comfort.

Dressing & grooming

Assistance choosing clothes, dressing, nail care, shaving, and personal styling — respecting individual preferences every time.

Continence management

Respectful, discreet support with continence care, including pad changes and catheter assistance where required.

Oral hygiene

Toothbrushing, denture care, and oral rinse assistance — often overlooked, always important for dignity and health.

Skin care & pressure monitoring

Daily moisturising routines and skin checks to identify early signs of pressure sores or skin breakdown.

Mobility & transfers

Safe assistance moving between bed, chair, and bathroom — using correct techniques to protect both client and carer.

Medication reminders

Prompting and supervising medication at the right times — supporting health routines without taking over independence.

Morning & evening routines

Full routine support at whatever time of day works best — structured around your schedule, not ours.

Who This Is For

Is personal care the right fit?

Personal care is right for anyone who wants to stay at home safely and independently — whatever the reason behind the need for support.

Older adults who want to stay at home

Ageing at home is a deeply personal choice. Personal care support enables your loved one to maintain their home life, their routines, and their independence — with a carer's helping hand for the parts that have become difficult.

People recovering from illness or surgery

Post-hospital recovery is the time when personal care support can make the biggest difference to outcomes. We provide short-term or ongoing personal care that allows people to recover at home safely, with proper hygiene and routine maintained throughout.

Families who need a trusted hand

Sometimes family members provide personal care until it becomes too physically demanding or emotionally difficult. Bringing in a professional doesn't mean stepping back — it means protecting the relationship. We take care of the morning routine so you can be there for the things only family can give.

Personal care is available through the Support at Home government program or as a private service. View our pricing or call us to discuss what funding you may be eligible for.

Common Questions

Personal care — your questions answered

Will I have the same carer each visit?

We do everything we can to ensure consistency. Where rosters allow, you'll see the same carer visit after visit. This matters to us — a familiar face builds trust, and trust makes good care possible. If a carer is unavailable, we'll always let you know in advance and introduce any replacement before they visit.

Can a male client request a male carer?

Absolutely. We respect personal preferences around gender for all personal care tasks. When you set up your care plan with us, simply let us know and we'll match you with an appropriate carer. Your comfort is not negotiable — it's a starting point.

What if my needs change over time?

Your care plan is a living document, not a fixed contract. As your situation changes — whether you need more support, less, or something different — we update your plan with you. Our team will regularly check in to make sure what we're delivering still fits your life.

How do I start personal care with J.PEER Health?

Call us on 0469 371 121 or complete the enquiry form on this page. We'll have a no-obligation friendly chat about what you need, then create a personalised care plan together. Most clients have care in place within a few days of their first conversation.

Is personal care covered by Support at Home?

Yes. Personal care is one of the core services funded through the Australian Government's Support at Home program. If you have an active Support at Home package, personal care can be included in your care plan at no or minimal cost to you. We can also provide personal care on a private, fee-for-service basis. Call us to discuss what's right for your situation.
