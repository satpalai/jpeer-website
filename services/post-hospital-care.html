What post-hospital care includes

Recovery at home requires more than rest. Our post-hospital care packages combine clinical support, personal care, and practical help — everything needed to recover safely and confidently at home.

Same-day or next-day care

Call us as soon as discharge is confirmed. We'll have a carer in place when your loved one arrives home — no waiting, no uncertainty.

Wound care & medication

Post-surgical wound monitoring and dressing changes, medication administration, and clinical observation — following the hospital discharge plan.

Personal care & mobility

Bathing, dressing, and mobility support adjusted to post-procedure limitations — with patience and professional technique.

Meals & nutrition

Recovery depends on nutrition. We prepare meals suited to post-procedure dietary requirements, including cultural food preferences.

Follow-up transport

Transport to post-discharge appointments, physio sessions, and specialist follow-ups — so nothing is missed in the recovery pathway.

GP & specialist liaison

We liaise with your hospital discharge team, GP, and specialists — keeping everyone informed and responding quickly to any clinical changes.

Why families choose J.PEER for post-hospital care

Speed matters most

Hospital discharge often happens with little notice. We respond fast. Call us the day before discharge and care starts the moment your loved one walks through the front door.

Language-matched from day one

Hospital discharge teams often can't arrange language-matched carers. We can. Recovery is stressful enough without navigating it in a second language.

Clinical and personal care combined

Post-hospital needs span both clinical and personal care. We provide both in a single coordinated package — so nothing falls through the gap between "medical" and "daily living."

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can J.PEER begin care after hospital discharge?

In most cases, we can begin care within 24 hours of discharge. If you contact us before the discharge date, we'll have everything in place so care starts the moment your loved one arrives home. Call us as soon as you have a discharge date confirmed.

Can I access post-hospital care privately while waiting for an assessment?

Yes. Private care can begin immediately, with no assessment required. This is ideal when someone is coming home from hospital and needs support right now. Once your Support at Home funding is confirmed, we can transition your care plan seamlessly.

Is post-hospital care covered by Support at Home?

Yes. Post-hospital and recovery support can be funded through your Support at Home budget. If you're not yet on a funded program, private care is available immediately and we can help you navigate the assessment process.

What if conditions deteriorate after coming home?

Our carers are trained to recognise warning signs and escalate appropriately. We maintain direct communication with your GP, and will arrange emergency services if required. You won't be managing a deterioration alone.

Can J.PEER help coordinate the hospital discharge itself?

Yes. We can liaise with the hospital discharge team, receive handover documentation, and coordinate with your GP and any specialists. We aim to make the transition from hospital to home as smooth and supported as possible.

Discharge is happening — call us now

Don't wait until discharge day. Call us now and we'll have care ready to go. Available 24 hours, 7 days a week, including public holidays.
