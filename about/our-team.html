Our Team

Meet the people behind your care

We hire for heart first, qualification second — then invest in training to make sure both are exceptional. Every carer on our team has been carefully selected, thoroughly screened, and given the cultural competence training to truly serve Melbourne's diverse communities.

The standard every team member meets

Before any carer visits your home, they've passed a rigorous process that most providers never bother with.

Police checked and screened

Every team member holds a current National Police Check and Working with Vulnerable People (WWVP) clearance — not as a tick-box exercise, but because your family's safety is non-negotiable.

Cultural competence training

All carers complete structured cultural sensitivity training before they begin. Language skills, dietary knowledge, religious observance, and communication styles — understood, not assumed.

Ongoing professional development

Care doesn't stand still — and neither does our team. Regular training in manual handling, first aid, dementia care, and the latest aged care standards keeps everyone at their best.

Faces you'll come to know

Consistent care means seeing familiar faces. We match carers to clients carefully — and we maintain that relationship.

Our standards

What we look for in every carer

Caring is a privilege. We take it seriously, which is why our hiring and vetting process is more thorough than most.

National Police Check

Current clearance required before any carer begins work with clients.

Working with Vulnerable People (WWVP) clearance

All carers hold current Victorian WWVP registration under the Worker Screening Act 2020.

First aid certification

Current first aid and CPR certification maintained by all care staff.

Manual handling training

Safe moving and handling techniques, including hoist operation where required.

Cultural sensitivity training

Structured training on cultural practices, dietary requirements, religious observance, and respectful communication across the communities we serve.

Reference and identity verification

Minimum two professional references checked, plus identity verification before offer of employment.

We also align with the requirements of the Aged Care Quality Standards under the Aged Care Act 2024, which set out the expectations for safe, respectful, and person-centred care in Australia.
