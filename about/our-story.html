Our Story

How a grandmother's story became our purpose

Some care providers are built to scale a business. J.PEER Health was built to right a wrong — a quiet, painful wrong that happens to thousands of families every year, and almost never makes the news.

Where it began

A family who knew care could be better

When our family moved to Australia, we brought everything that mattered — our language, our traditions, our food, and our sense of how family cares for family. What we didn't bring, and what we weren't prepared for, was a care system that struggled to meet us halfway.

Our grandmother needed home care. She was proud, fiercely independent, and she spoke only Hindi. The carers who visited were kind — genuinely kind — but they couldn't communicate with her. Meals arrived that she couldn't eat. Her morning routine, so important to her sense of dignity, was rushed and changed. Her questions went unanswered, not because no one cared, but because no one understood.

The situation wasn't caused by cruelty. It was caused by a system that had never been designed with someone like her in mind. And when you have 45+ years of aged care experience between your family, as we do, that realisation doesn't sit quietly. It asks something of you.

J.PEER Health was born from that question: what would care look like if it was truly built for her?

The answer is what you'll find here. Care delivered in your language. Carers who understand your traditions, your food, your faith, your routines. A team with the experience to know what excellent care actually looks like — not just care that passes a checklist, but care that genuinely feels like home.
